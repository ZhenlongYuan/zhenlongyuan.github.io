- ***🦾 Embodied Agents:***
How can we build embodied agents that perceive, understand, and interact with the physical world? My research focuses on endowing foundation models with spatial reasoning capabilities, enabling them to actively explore environments, manipulate objects, and make decisions in complex 3D spaces.

- ***🗺️ Spatial Intelligence:***
How can machines develop a deep understanding of 3D space and spatial relationships? I explore ways to enhance models' spatial reasoning, enabling them to reason about object positions, distances, and geometric transformations in real-world scenarios.

- ***🚀 Foundation Model:***
How can we develop large-scale models that generalize across diverse tasks? I am interested in pre-training strategies, architecture innovations, and scaling laws that enable foundation models to achieve strong performance with minimal task-specific adaptation.

- ***🏥 AI for Science:***
How can AI accelerate scientific discovery? My work investigates how generative models and multimodal learning can assist in scientific research, from molecular design to physical simulation.

- ***👁️ 3D Vision:***
How can machines perceive and reconstruct 3D environments from 2D images? I work on multi-view stereo, depth estimation, and 3D reconstruction techniques that enable accurate geometric understanding of the world.

- ***🛡️ Safety:***
How can we ensure AI systems are safe and reliable? I research alignment techniques, robustness evaluation, and risk mitigation strategies to build trustworthy AI systems.
